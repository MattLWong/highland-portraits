About Me...

Matt L. Wong (b. 1990) is a portrait photographer based in Berkeley CA. His style is influenced by the last two generations of portrait photographers, including Timothy Greenfield-Sanders, Annie Leibovitz, and Richard Avedon. He uses a combination of large-format and digital sensors. He received his B.S. in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of California at Davis.
